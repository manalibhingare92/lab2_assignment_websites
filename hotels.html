HOTELS IN PUNE

1. Hyatt Pune

Hyatt Pune is a part of the incredible world of Hyatt. Located just 10 minutes from Pune Airport, 5 minutes from Phoenix Market City, and 15 minutes from the IT hub, Hyatt Pune has the perfect location, whether you’re traveling for work or leisure. Take advantage of our world-class Shanti spa where Ayurvedic treatments, reiki, and yoga relax and re-energize you. There’s something for everyone, with a scenic outdoor pool, garden suites, and round-the-clock room service, you will be spoilt for choice. Our three in-house restaurants- Baan Tao, Café 88, The Terrace Bar and TG's make dining decadent. Baan Tao is an award-winning restaurant, famed for its Thai and Chinese cuisine complemented by the ambiance of unique open-to-sky Thai Sala. If you’re in the mood for alfresco to enjoy Pune’s famously pleasant year-round weather, our restaurants are the perfect picks for you.

2. Comfort Inn Baner

Comfort Inn Baner is known for its modern amenities and exceptional service. Located in the vibrant Baner area, it offers easy access to major business hubs and entertainment spots in Pune. The hotel features well-appointed rooms with contemporary decor, a fitness center, and a multi-cuisine restaurant. Guests can enjoy a relaxing stay with facilities like free Wi-Fi, complimentary breakfast, and a business center.

3. Centro

Centro offers a stylish stay with a range of facilities designed for both leisure and business travelers. Located in a prime area, Centro provides easy access to local attractions and transport options. Guests can enjoy a range of services including comfortable rooms, a well-equipped fitness center, and a dining option that serves delicious meals. The hotel is known for its welcoming ambiance and excellent customer service.
